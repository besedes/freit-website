About FREIT

An international organization dedicated to furthering research in empirical international trade.

Our mission

FREIT has been developed as an umbrella organization for conferences for empirical international trade researchers around the world.

The Forum organizes annual conferences across North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia, bringing together leading researchers in the field at every stage of their careers.

FREIT is based at the Georgia Institute of Technology, where its operations and conference coordination are managed.
